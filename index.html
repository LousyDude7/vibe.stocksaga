Game Rules

Objective

Grow your virtual investment portfolio by making smart stock trades in response to market fluctuations and news events.

Starting Balance

Each team begins with $100,000 in virtual currency to invest.

Stock Trading

  • You can buy and sell stocks at current market prices
  • Stock prices update every 5 seconds with random fluctuations
  • News events will significantly impact certain stock prices
  • No transaction fees or taxes are applied

News Events

The admin will periodically release news events that affect specific stocks. Pay attention to the news ticker for these announcements.

Winning

The team with the highest portfolio value (cash + stock value) at the end of the game session wins.
